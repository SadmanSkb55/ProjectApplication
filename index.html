Privacy Policy for ProjectApplication

Last Updated: 3/8/2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your data when you use our application to integrate Gmail and Telegram services. We prioritize user privacy and ensure that no personal data is stored externally.

2. Data Collection & Usage

Gmail & Telegram Data Access:

  • The app requires access to your Gmail and Telegram accounts only to automate workflows as defined by you.
  • We do not store, share, or transmit your emails, messages, or personal data to any external servers.
  • All processing occurs locally on your device unless explicitly required for a specific feature (which will be disclosed).

No External Storage:

  • Your data remains within your Gmail/Telegram accounts and is never logged, copied, or retained by us.

3. Permissions & Security

OAuth Scopes:

  • The app requests necessary permissions (e.g., gmail.readonly, telegram.messages) solely to perform user-authorized actions.

Security Measures:

  • All API calls are encrypted (HTTPS).
  • We do not cache credentials or sensitive data beyond the session duration.

4. Third-Party Services

Google & Telegram APIs:

  • The app relies on these services, and their own privacy policies apply to data processed by them.
  • We do not control how Google or Telegram handle your data outside our app.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy and will notify users via the app or website. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
